COMPANY OVERVIEW

Yes Bank Ltd offers a full-range of client-focused corporate banking services, including working capital finance, specialized corporate finance, trade and transactional services, treasury risk management services, investment banking solutions and liquidity management solutions among others to a highly focused client base. The bank also has a widespread branch network of over 331 branches across 200 cities, with over 420 ATM`s and 2 National Operating Centres in Mumbai and Gurgaon.The business areas of the bank includes:Corporate and Institutional Banking,Business Banking,Corporate Finance,Retail Banking and Investment Banking.
